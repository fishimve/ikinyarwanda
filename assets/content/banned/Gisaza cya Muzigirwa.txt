Uwo Abangamugayo basingiza
Ko asigana abandi inshuro
Wa Rusengo
Mu ishokero rya Rutigita
Abaje kumvuna
Basanze uwo twavuganaga
Avira amatete
Mu mataba y'u Mwogere.
Nishe Karyega mu Gikingo
Nishe Ntizigende mu Gikingo
Nishe Rukimirana mu Gikingo
Nishe umututsi w'amenyo abiri
Twaturukije Inkikabahizi.
Nishe umututsi w'uruziga
Mu nzira y'Abatoyi.
Niciye mu Karico
Abahebyi batashye.
